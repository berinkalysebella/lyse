Lesson 3: Numbers in English

Learn how to say numbers from 1 to 10 in English:

  • One :Un / Rimwe
  • Two :Deux / Kabiri
  • Three :Trois / Gatatu
  • Four :Quatre / Kane
  • Five :Cinq / Gatanu
  • Six :Six / Gatandatu
  • Seven :Sept / Indwi
  • Eight :Huit / Umunani
  • Nine :Neuf / Icenda
  • Ten :Dix / Icumi
