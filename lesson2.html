Lesson 2: Colors in English

Here are some basic colors in English with their French/Kirundi meanings:

  • Red: Rouge
  • Blue : Bleu
  • Green : Vert
  • Yellow : Jaune
  • Black : Noir
  • White : Blanc
  • Orange : Orange
